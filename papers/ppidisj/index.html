Towards redefining anti-licensing conditions on positive polarity items: Evidence from PPI-disjunctions in Russian.

Rudnev, Pavel. In preparation. Towards redefining anti-licensing conditions on positive polarity items: Evidence from PPI-disjunctions in Russian.

Most prominent accounts of polarity-sensitive disjunctions treat positive polarity as an inherently semantic phenomenon based on the notions of downward entailment (Szabolcsi 2002, 2004) or referentiality (Giannakidou 2011). In this note I present novel data from Russian to argue that these analyses fail to take into account the relevance of syntactic factors.
