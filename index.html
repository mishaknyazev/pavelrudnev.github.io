Pavel Rudnev

I’m a linguist specialising in the interaction between syntactic structure and semantic interpretation. I got my PhD from the University of Groningen in 2015. Languages I’ve worked on include Turkish, Avar (Northeast Caucasian) and Russian.

Originally I’m from Zlatoust (Russia), and if I’m not doing linguistics, chances are you’ll find me baking some heritage Eastern European rye bread.
