Syntax and semantics: Capita Selecta

Ā-dependencies

Autumn 2012

Advanced undergraduate topical course, University of Groningen.

 lecture notes

This course presents an in-depth examination of the phenomenon of Ā-dependencies and its role in contemporary syntactic and semantic theory. It has three main objectives:

  • knowledge of basic concepts of syntactic and semantic theory
  • ability to read and assess research articles dealing with a particular empirical phenomenon
  • understanding how detailed examination of an empirical phenomenon can inform theoretical linguistic work

It is aimed at developing the following competencies:

  • identification of research problems and theoretical questions
  • critical discussion of research literature
  • structured and coherent writing
  • summarizing and presenting a scientific topic
